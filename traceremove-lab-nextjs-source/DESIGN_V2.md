# Visual system v2

This revision moves the project away from a generic dark academic template and toward a distinct research-instrument identity.

## Public site

- Asymmetric editorial hero with an interactive research constellation.
- Stronger thesis-led copy rather than generic “research directions”.
- Indexed research architecture instead of repeated SaaS cards.
- Interactive, transparent public diagnostic with four risk dimensions.
- Laboratory consoles for inquiry lenses, concept architecture, and assessment protocol.
- Searchable and filterable publication archive with transparent status labels.
- Stronger institutional audit and commercial access pages.

## Private workspace

- Separate product shell without the public navigation.
- Case register rather than a generic projects table.
- Structured case-definition workflow.
- Evidence register and assessment operation console.
- Version rail, visual risk score, findings register, responsibility chain, and next-action record.

## Principles

- No fabricated statistics, certifications, institutional affiliations, or publication status.
- The public diagnostic explicitly identifies itself as a rule-based heuristic.
- AI-assisted findings remain subject to human review.
- The visual language is editorial, analytical, and operational rather than decorative.
